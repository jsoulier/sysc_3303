=== Requirements ===

- IntelliJ IDEA
- JDK
- PDF viewer

=== Setup Instructions ===

1. Open IntelliJ IDEA
2. Click File > Open
3. Enter the directory of Assignment1_101189819
4. Open the project explorer
5. Right click on src/Main.java
6. Click "Run Main.main()"
